CrystalsCollector!

The number in the GREEN box is the RANDOM NUMBER.

Your goal is to match the TOTAL SCORE with the RANDOM NUMBER. You will do this by utilizing a combination of the magic crystals below.

Each CRYSTAL has a randomized SCORE value assigned to it. This SCORE is reset after each game.

If your TOTAL SCORE exceeds the RANDOM NUMBER, you lose the game. If you match the TOTAL SCORE with the RANDOM NUMBER, you win the game.
